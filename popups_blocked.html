Chat, prompts, or new windows are not opening in mySWS

Condition

  • When you click a link in mySWS to go to chat, your document, or a new page, nothing happens.
  • You are not recieiving prompts or confirmation pop-up windows in mySWS.

Cause

You have prevented pop-ups or browser dialog windows from showing up.

Solution

  • Turn off your pop-up blocker in your browser's settings while using mySWS.
  • Turn off any ad blockers installed on your browser or computer while using mySWS.
